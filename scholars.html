Scholars & Students of Knowledge

A developing list of scholars and students of knowledge who contribute to the preservation and teaching of the Sunni Ashʿarī tradition. This directory is not exhaustive and will grow over time, in shā’ Allāh.

Shaykh Ḥārūn Kanj

A respected teacher of Ashʿarī creed and Shāfiʿī fiqh, known for his clarity and reliability in presenting Sunni doctrine.

Shaykh Abū Mālik al-Minhājī

A teacher specializing in classical creed texts and foundational theology, focusing on the mainstream Sunni scholastic tradition.

Shaykh Shams Tameez

A UK-based scholar known for his structured teaching of Ashʿarī creed, logic, and traditional Islamic sciences.

Shaykh Mubeen Raza

A dedicated student of knowledge and teacher involved in various Ashʿarī educational initiatives across the English-speaking world.

Shaykh Saʿīd Foudah

A leading contemporary defender of Ashʿarī kalām, authoring numerous works refuting anthropomorphism and reviving classical usūl.
