Guiding Principles

  • Academic integrity: Every refutation is based on classical Sunni scholarship and verified sources.
  • Respectful discourse: We address ideas — not individuals or groups — with dignity and fairness.
  • Preservation of scholarly heritage: Our methodology follows the Ashʿarī, Māturīdī, and Sunni tradition upheld for over a millennium.
  • Reliance on verified sources: Arguments are grounded in Qurʾān, Hadith, and authenticated statements of recognized scholars.
